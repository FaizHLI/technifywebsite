ABOUT US

Technify is proudly funded by the United States Department of State as a part of their Young Southeast Asian Leaders Initiative. We hope to make a difference in Southeast Asia, a rapidly growing region with economic and social potential matched by few other regions.

Technify's other sponsors include: The Duke Innovation & Entrepreneurship Innovation Co-Lab and Cultural Vistas.

Technify Members have gone on to work at companies such as Google, Amazon, Meta, McKinsey & Company, and Bain & Company, while continuing to serve non-profits around the globe.
