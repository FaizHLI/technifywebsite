NAU Foundation Website

Developed a website for the Paryavaran Shala initiative by the NAU Foundation to support nature-based solutions in education.

  • Implemented a research repository for accessible documentation on nature-based solutions.
  • Created a section for case studies to provide replicable examples of successful interventions.
  • Documented pilot projects with detailed information on challenges and outcomes.
  • Integrated geotagging to map schools suitable for interventions and visualize potential impact.

Coding for Social Good Nepal

Designed and deployed a website for CoSoG, a nonprofit promoting computer science education in Nepal.

  • Developed pages for general information about the organization and its mission.
  • Showcased ongoing projects and news updates.
  • Created a resource hub for event schedules and educational materials.
  • Collaborated with executives to design a functional and user-friendly interface.
